What it does

Built around your
tradition.

Every community has its own minhag. SixHeaven adapts — not the other way around.

🥩

Meat → Dairy

Defaults to the classic 6-hour wait. Also supports a full 5-hour wait, or "רוב שש" (5h 31m) for those who follow that custom. Need something else? Set any duration you like.

6 hours 5 hours רוב שש Custom
🥛

Dairy → Meat

Track the dairy-to-meat wait with equal precision. Choose 30 minutes, 1 hour, or the full 6-hour wait — all within the same clean interface.

30 min 1 hour 6 hours Custom
🔔

Smart Integration

Persistent notifications keep your timer visible at all times. Exact alarms fire precisely when your wait is up. Toggle from the Quick Settings tile — no need to even open the app.

Persistent Notif. Exact Alarm Widgets Quick Tile
📲

Home & Lock Screen Widgets

Check your remaining wait at a glance without unlocking your phone. The countdown widget sits beautifully on any home screen layout.

🔓

Open Source & Ad-Free

No subscriptions, no data collection, no ads. SixHeaven is fully open source — you can inspect every line of code on GitHub.
