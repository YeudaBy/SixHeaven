Privacy Policy for SixHeaven
Last Updated: April 2026

Your privacy is paramount. SixHeaven is built with a "Privacy by Design" philosophy.

1. Data Collection
SixHeaven does not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information. We do not use trackers, analytics, or third-party advertising SDKs.

2. Permissions Used
The app requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality:

Notifications: To display the active timer and alert you when your waiting time is over.

Exact Alarms: To ensure the timer is accurate to the second, even when the device is in power-saving mode.

3. Data Storage
All app settings (your chosen wait times and active timers) are stored locally on your device. This data is never uploaded to any server and is deleted if you uninstall the app.

4. Third-Party Services
The app is entirely independent. There are no logins, social media integrations, or cloud backups that could compromise your anonymity.

5. Open Source Transparency
Since SixHeaven is open-source, you are welcome to inspect our source code on GitHub to verify our privacy claims.
